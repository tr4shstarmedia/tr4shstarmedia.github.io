My Story

Hey everyone, it’s great to be back!

First, let me start out by saying a huge thank you to all my fans and everyone who gave 200% to the search effort and raised awareness for me. You’re the best fans a clipper could ask for. 🥺🙏

Now, for the elephant in the room. I do have amnesia, so I’m only able to tell you about what I was doing at the time I was kidnapped, and a bit about after I woke up in the hospital.

I was walking down the strip in Vegas, taking pictures with fans and enjoying some downtime before my next clipping session. I dropped by a couple stores, Louis, Chanel, Dolce, Prada, etc. So I was walking down the street with like 10 bags full of luxury goods, when a fan recognized me and asked me if I needed a ride.

My special edition Lambo was in the shop, so I caught a ride to the strip on my friend JBoss925’s private helicopter. JBoss was still shopping, and there was no way I was getting an Uber at that hour on the strip, so I accepted the fan’s offer. I even tossed them a Louis bag as a thank you, just a little something for their troubles.

The fan dropped me off at the lobby of my Penthouse, and I thanked them and gave them a selfie with me. I took the private elevator up to my penthouse, and it opened up into my living room. I bent down to grab my bags and felt something hit my neck. It stung and sent an icy feeling through my veins. I felt something like a feather on my neck and pulled out a dart. My vision was fading, I couldn’t feel my hands anymore; it was like I was staring down a tunnel. I looked up and saw a figure standing in my living room, then nothing.

Suddenly, it was all white. I was confused, I had no sense of where my body was. I thought I was dead. I squinted, my eyes were burning, and suddenly a white ceiling tile came into focus. I could feel my body again, it felt like I was floating. I looked to my right and saw a heart monitor, so I tried to sit up. I almost passed out so I layed back down. There were at least 5 IVs in my arms. I flipped onto my left side, and saw a call button zip tied onto the bed’s railing. I wrenched my wrist, pressed it, and a team of doctors ran in, surprised to see me awake. I slipped in and out of consciousness as they tried to speak to me. I couldn’t muster the strength to say anything back.

Then I met God. Or what I think was God, at least. It was like a huge, beautiful white monster in the sky, miles across at least. It had no form I could recognize, seemingly ebbing and flowing with the wind. It had no mouth, but I could hear it clearly. It told me that I was its son, that I was betrayed. It told me I was special, that I was the world. That the universe exists only because I command it to, and that once I cease, so too will the universe. That it and I are the same. I felt it rip my soul out of my body and fling me into the cosmos. It was so incredibly loud, the vacuum whistling in my ears, and then nothing again.

A couple of hours later, I woke up again. I felt groggy. I opened my eyes and saw a nurse in the room. She heard my rustling, and immediately shot over. She asked me what my name was. “Jacky”, I said. Her eyes lit up, and she called in the neurologist.

The neurologist told me they weren’t sure I would ever wake up. They said I had no brain activity, that I shouldn’t even be alive. I sat in the bed for hours before feeling well enough to even sit up. My whole body ached, but the staff assured me I would be okay. I was scared to fall asleep, fearing I’d never wake up.

Days passed, and I regained much of my strength. I’m still not 100%, my head feels foggy, but I’m slowly becoming my old self again. I keep getting these visions at night, I don’t know what they mean. But for now, I’m just happy to be alive.

Thanks for listening,

JackyBoii76
